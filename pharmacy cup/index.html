What is Ph.CUP
Ph.CUP is a yearly football tournament where teams consisting of teachers, staff, and students from the College of Pharmacy-Al-Mustansiriya University participate. The tournament takes place at the college stadium and is supervised by the Student Activities Department in collaboration with student volunteers.

About us

Our Goals
Enhancing the experience of students and employees at the college, promoting a sense of belonging among them, and creating a lasting legacy and memories. As the saying goes, "a sound mind is in a sound body," hence this tournament aims to improve the physical and mental well-being of the participants. Boost the self-confidence of students and give them hope in developing their athletic talent while pursuing their academic studies.

Our Message
The tournament's message is crystal clear; it aims to communicate the notion that the teachers, employees and students of the College of Pharmacy possess additional talents and abilities beyond their academic and occupational responsibilities. Their contributions to society extend far beyond their job description.

Our Mission
Our Mission is to organize a professional and distinguished tournament that is worthy of the reputation and history of the College of Pharmacy at Al-Mustansiriya University. Additionally, we aim to prepare a team of organizers, photographers, designers, and programmers who are capable of managing various forums, events, and conferences both inside and outside the college.
